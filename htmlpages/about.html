About Us

This platform is designed to streamline the attendance tracking process for educationalinstitutions.To begin,colleges must first register,providing their essential information,including collegename, contact details,and a uniqueidentifier such as a college ID.Once registered,users can choosetheir role,by selecting their college either as a lecturer or a student.Lecturers have the ability to take attendance efficiently through this platform.They can create and manage class sessions,mark attendance for each student,and keep a digital record of attendance history.This feature not only simplifies the attendance-taking process also reduces paperwork,allowing lecturers to focus more on the educational aspects of their role.On the other hand, students can access the platform to view their attendance records. By logging in with their credentials,students gain real-time insights into their attendance percentages,providing them with a transparent overview of their class attendance.This empowers students to take responsibility for their attendance and stay informed about their academic progress.
